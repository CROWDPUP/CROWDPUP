About CrowdPup

CrowdPup is a groundbreaking meme token designed to combine the fun and community-driven spirit of meme culture with the powerful capabilities of blockchain technology. This combination opens up new possibilities for both creators and investors, allowing anyone, anywhere, to start or support crowdfunding campaigns using cryptocurrency.

Tokenomics

  • Total Supply: 100 billion $PUP tokens
  • Allocation: Airdrops, liquidity pools, platform development, and marketing
  • Usage: Pay fees, invest in campaigns, and reward active community members

Roadmap

  1. Q1 2024:
    **Token Launch & Beta Version of the Platform**

    The official launch of $PUP token, alongside an initial distribution via airdrops. We will launch the beta version of the CrowdPup platform, allowing early adopters to create and support crowdfunding campaigns. During this phase, we will gather feedback from the community to refine and improve the platform.

  2. Q2 2024:
    **Mainstream Platform Integration & Partnerships**

    We will partner with existing crowdfunding platforms to integrate $PUP as an accepted currency for funding projects. Additionally, we will focus on expanding our partnerships with other DeFi projects to further increase the utility of $PUP. The goal is to increase platform adoption and ensure widespread use of our token.

  3. Q3 2024:
    **Launch of Mobile App & Staking System**

    The launch of a mobile app for easier campaign management, enabling users to track campaigns and make contributions directly from their smartphones. Additionally, we will introduce a staking system that allows users to earn rewards by holding and staking $PUP tokens, incentivizing long-term participation in the ecosystem.

  4. Q4 2024:
    **Global Expansion & Continuous Improvements**

    We will expand the CrowdPup platform into new markets worldwide. This expansion will be accompanied by continuous improvements and feature additions based on user feedback. Our goal is to grow CrowdPup into a global decentralized crowdfunding platform that is accessible to anyone, anywhere. We will also continue integrating with other DeFi projects to enhance the utility of $PUP and expand its reach.
