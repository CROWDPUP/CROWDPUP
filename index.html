About CrowdPup

CrowdPup ($PUP) is a meme token with a serious mission. It allows anyone to start crowdfunding campaigns through a decentralized platform, powered by blockchain and cryptocurrency. Our goal is to make crowdfunding easier, faster, and more accessible to everyone, with low fees and no intermediaries. Using $PUP tokens, you can create campaigns, fund projects, and earn rewards as you help grow these campaigns.

How CrowdPup Works

Launch a Campaign

Create a crowdfunding campaign using $PUP tokens. Set your funding goal and offer rewards for your backers.

Invest in Projects

Invest in campaigns that interest you. The more you contribute, the more you earn in $PUP.

Decentralized and Transparent

All transactions are transparent on the blockchain, giving you full control and security.
